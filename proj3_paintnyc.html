Paint NYC

Back

PaintNYC was made using Python and the Pygame library and was an exercise in UX/UI and implementing cool functionalities. I chose the theme of New York because I had just come back from a family trip and was inspired by the multitudes of artistic expression found there.

The software has many drawing tools, backgrounds, stickers and a file save/load system, making it interesting and fun!
