Midnight Sun is a design team that builds a solar powered vehicle from scratch. My role is on the firmware sub-team, focusing on the GUI for the driver control and data display. The GUI is constructed with React.js to manage the components and HTML/CSS for styling.

Midnight Sun has been a great learning experience for the past 2 months and I am highly anticipating the completion of the car in 2020. One of the most valuable aspects for me has been the amazing team of people. I have already learned A LOT about how you go about building an entire solar powered car and the team leads provide great guidance. I'm currently learning React, MongoDB, and Flask to develop the driver control panel. It's a great time!
